Welcome to

UNORSSGO ARCHIVES!

MISSION

To preserve, organize, and make accessible the historical records and documents of the University of Negros Occidental-Recoletos Supreme Student Government for future generations, fostering transparency and institutional memory.

VISION

To be the premier digital repository that effectively documents student leadership initiatives, ensuring a comprehensive historical record of student governance that inspires future leaders and promotes accountability.

"As we launch the UNORSSGO Archives, we commit to preserving our organization's rich history and achievements. This platform will serve as both our legacy and our compass, documenting our journey while guiding future student leaders. I invite all students to explore, contribute, and take pride in our collective accomplishments."

Hon. Denver S. Fernandez

UNORSSGO President
